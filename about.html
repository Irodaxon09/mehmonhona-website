About Our Hotel

The hotel serves guests a sweet continental breakfast from 7:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m., which is included in the price of the stay. The menu offers various dishes and drinks of European and Uzbek cuisine. In addition, you can order a "business lunch" during the day, and a light dinner in the evening.

A number of hotel services are offered to guests: upon prior request, organization of money transfer from the airport, railway station and in the opposite direction, cleaning of clothes: washing and ironing of clothes; reservation and delivery of air and railway tickets; organization of guided tours; guarded parking lot; separate rooms for luggage storage
